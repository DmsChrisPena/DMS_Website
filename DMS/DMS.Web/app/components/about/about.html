Our Mission

Our industry has always been plagued by a gap between demand for industry experts and the supply of a skilled labor force; we see this gap as increasing in the future. The gas industry is on the course to rapidly grow particularly due to recent developments in fracking and LNG technology. The primary problem is that companies still try, buy and implement solutions like they did in the 80's.

Our mission here at DMS is simple. We are here to provide effective and easy-to-implement solutions that close this gap, without costing millions of dollars.

  • Our products automate your processes, filling the need for additional personnel as you significantly increase your production
  • Our services save you time, eliminating the need for in-house brute-force accounting, scheduling, consulting and training
  • Our educational programs boost your personnel's knowledge, keeping your organization continually ahead of the curve

Founding

In 1993, a group of pipeline, accounting and mathematic experts gathered to create a better and seamless way to manage the business functions of companies operating in this emerging sector. This group formed Data Management Solutions.

University of Houston

One of our biggest sources of pride is our affiliation with the Univeristy of Houston

That's why Frank Pena, CEO of DMS, has taught an upper-level course dedicated to Energy Trading Systems at UH since 2010. He has reached Supply Chain, Finance, MIS and Accounting majors as well as MIS graduate students.

The Course

  • Knowledge: In one semester, students are exposed to 16 weeks of hard-hitting lectures. We discuss the nitty-gritty details of the gas industry, telling the whole story of a unit of gas as it travels from wellhead to end-user.
  • Practice: The class is as much hands-on as it is lecture-oriented. All students are provided with their own licenses of our ETRM systems, GasPro and GasTrak and have labs that accomopany the lectures. They input their own source information, trade buy/sells, schedule including single- and multi-leg transactions, as well as pool, store and transfer gas.

The Success

  • Since 2010, course enrollment has grown from 16 to 185+ students.
  • Students have reported that in interviews with big-name Oil & Gas companies in Houston, they had been asked, "Have you taken the Energy Trading Systems course taught by Frank Pena at UH?"
  • We continually receive notes from students how much of an impact the course has made in jump-starting their careers.
