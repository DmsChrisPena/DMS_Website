Founding

In 1993, a group of pipeline, accounting and mathematic experts gathered to create a better and seamless way to manage the business functions of companies operating in this emerging sector. This group formed Data Management Solutions.

Founder

Frank Peña, C.P.A. and CEO of DMS, has been in the gas industry for over 30 years. Prior to forming DMS, he:

  • Co-chaired the Southern Gas Association, representing United Gas Pipeline (now Koch Industires) and also Northern Natural Gas PipelineTestified before FERC as an expert witness on gas accounting and PGA issues

  • Created, structured and managed previously non-existent control groups at NNG, in areas such as finance, transportation imbalances, storage, fuel consumption and regulatory reporting requirements

Today

Our organization is a blend of people who have been here from the beginning and young, new ambitious people. Our team boasts a broad yet rich background, with education and experience including forensic and gas accounting, computer science, economics, finance, marketing, supply-chain and advanced mathematics.

We pool our resources together solely to provide you with the most effective and comprehensive solutions currently available to the industry.
