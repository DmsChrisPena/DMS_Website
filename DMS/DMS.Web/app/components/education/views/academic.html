College Courses

Frank Pena, CEO of DMS, has taught an upper-level course dedicated to Energy Trading Systems at UH since 2010. He has reached Supply Chain, Finance, MIS and Accounting majors as well as MIS graduate students.

Since 2010, the course has grown from 16 to 185+ students. We cover the ins and outs of the industry and tell the story of of gas from wellhead to end-user.

If you happen to be a UH student, take our course! We would love to have you. We are looking to expand to other colleges in the area such as Rice, A&M and UT.
