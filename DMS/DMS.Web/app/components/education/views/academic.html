Online Certifications

Need to show to potential employers that you know the ins and outs of the gas industry? Looking to grow your knowledge from your own area of expertise to an industry-wide view?

College Courses

Frank Pena, CEO of DMS, has taught an upper-level course dedicated to Energy Trading Systems at UH since 2010. The course has primarily reached Supply Chain, Finance, MIS and Accounting majors as well as MIS graduate students.

Since 2010, the course has grown from 16 to 185+ students per semester. We cover the ins and outs of the industry and tell the story of gas from wellhead to end-user.

If you happen to be a UH student, take our course! We would love to have you. We are looking to expand to other colleges such as Rice, A&M and UT.
